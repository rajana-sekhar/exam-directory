Eligible for Exam

1. Indian citizen

2. Studying in Class 10th

3. Not enrolled in distance learning, correspondence courses or graduate programs

Important Documents

• Caste certificate

• PwD certificate in case of physically challenged candidate

• Academic certificate

Procedure

• Open the official website of the Department of Science and Technology (DST), Government of India

• From the home page of the website click “application” option

• Then click “click here for registration” option

• Fill the registration form by entering a name, email id, contact number and captcha code

• Click the “register” option and then fill the rest application form with personal and academic details

• Upload the image, signature and other mandatory documents in the prescribed form

• Submit the application form after review of the entered details

• Make a payment of the application form and take a print out of the application form and payment receipt.
