Eligible for Exam:

• Age Limit: There is no upper age limit to register for the GMAT exam. However, there is a lower ceiling that candidates must be 18 years or older for taking the GMAT exam. Candidates between 13 and 17 years of age can also register for the GMAT exam after providing a signed GMAT Parental Consent/Authorization form (NOC from legal guardians or parents).

• Language Requirements: Students with any native language can register for the GMAT exam. There is no compulsion that their schooling or higher education must be from English medium only. Academic Background: When it comes to GMAT registration vis-à-vis a student’s academic background, students do not require any minimum academic qualification to register for the GMAT exam. In addition, there are no restrictions related to your undergraduate degree marks.

• Work Experience: Students do not need any minimum work experience to register for the GMAT exam. It is vital to note that MBA colleges might have certain eligibility requirements. Students are advised to note the MBA admission requirements for different colleges before applying.
