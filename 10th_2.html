Eligible for Exam

Student must have passed 9th exam with a score of 60% or higher and is enrolled in class 10th

Important Documents

• A passport-sized photograph of the applicant

• Aadhaar Card of the applicant

• Domicile/ residential certificate of the applicant

• Income certificate of the applicant’s family

• Caste and disability certificate

• The applicant’s class 9 mark sheet

Procedure

Step 1: Navigate to the NCERT portal in your state or union territory. The applicant can also contact his state Liaison Officer.

Step 2: Upload all the required documents after filling out the application form. In addition, the school’s principal or head must attest to the application.

Step 3: Make the payment and submit the form.
